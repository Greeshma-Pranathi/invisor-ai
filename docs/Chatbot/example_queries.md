## Churn-Related Queries
- Which customers are likely to churn?
- How many customers are at high churn risk?
- What features influence churn the most?
- Why is this customer at risk of churn? (when selected)

## Customer Segmentation Queries
- What customer segments exist?
- Explain segment 2.
- What defines segment 1?
- How many customers are in each segment?

## Dataset-Level Insight Queries
- Which segment has the highest churn risk?
- Are some segments more churn-prone?
- How does churn vary across segments?

## Unsupported Queries
- What-if scenarios
- Model retraining or configuration
- Causal explanations
- Prescriptive business advice

The chatbot only summarizes existing machine learning outputs and does
not perform new analysis or reasoning.
